# Exp17 Preregistered Predictions

## Replication hypotheses

- `R1`: Lu-style Assistant Axis structure remains visible on most of our six
  PT/IT families, even if its strength is scale-sensitive.
- `R2`: PT and IT Assistant Axis directions are more similar than PT and IT
  refusal directions.
- `R3`: Du-style truthfulness directions are more cross-variant transferable
  than refusal directions.
- `R4`: The strongest layers for these external behavioral directions are
  concentrated in the middle-to-late stack, not restricted to the earliest band.

## Synthesis hypotheses

- `S1`: At least some externally replicated behavior-related directions align
  more closely with the convergence-gap peak or the late control region than a
  "pure middle-layer only" reading would predict.
- `S2`: Any overlap is strongest as a middle-to-late circuit story rather than
  as evidence that the exact same single direction fully explains the
  convergence gap.
